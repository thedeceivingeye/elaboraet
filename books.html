How to Read a Book
(1940) - (1972)

 Mortimer J. Adler; Charles Van Doren. A key to learning from other books: one of the first steps in pursuing your own education. It is recommended you read the first edition before reading the most up-to-date version as the 1972 edition was a completely re-written edition with a slightly different focus and writing style. But both editions are highly recommended.
The Meaning of a Liberal Education Everett Dean Martin Published 1926, written by Everett Dean Martin. Can be found on archive.org for free as a PDF, among other formats.
The Lost Tools of Learning Dorothy Sayers A valuable and famous essay by Sayers, highly recommended. Can be found online for free, such as on gbt.org. Because of its free availability, it is recommended you download it onto your computer for convenience.
