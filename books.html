Classics
Title Author Information
How to Read a Book Mortimer J. Adler; Charles Van Doren. It is recommended you read the first edition before reading the most up-to-date version as the 1972 edition was a completely re-written edition with a slightly different focus and writing style. But both editions are highly recommended.
The Meaning of a Liberal Education Everett Dean Martin A bit rare, and is almost impossible to find online. It is contained on archive.org, but it seems to only offer "daisy" files for borrowing.
The Lost Tools of Learning Dorothy Sayers Can be found online for free, such as on gbt.org. Because of its free availability, it is recommended you download it onto your computer for convenience.
