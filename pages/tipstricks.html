Useful Tricks

This page contains a small collection of what I consider to be "tips and tricks," or generally useful ways of doing many things. This page may not be updated often.


  • If you're tired of copying and pasting Em Dashes or En Dashes and are on Windows, you can simply enter ALT+0151 (from your numpad) to insert an em dash (—) or ALT+0150 to insert an en dash (–). For comparison, here is an ordinary hyphen commonly used to represent subtraction: (-).
