About Elaboraet

Elaboraet is a site dedicated to the authoring and collection of articles.

The Goal of Elaboraet

As ambitious as it sounds, I plan to make Elaboraet a more-or-less extensive, original virtual catalog of articles both written by me and by others. The articles by me are currently listed under the Original Articles category, and the articles that I have linked—written by others—are under the Recommended Readings category.

A History of Elaboraet - Wondering why this site is called 'Elaboraet'?

Writing Quality or "Prose Style"

I'm a learning writer! My current goal is to achieve a style in which I efficiently convey my meaning without 'fluff' or extraneous words. If you'd like to provide suggestions or critique on any of my writing, feel free to post a message or email me.

Notice: Being a hobby site, the information presented by me, although I will try to be accurate at all times, may not be wholly standard or even correct; I'm only one person, am not a professional, and do not earn any money for doing this. Likewise, this being a hobby, I probably won't build the site as if I depend on it—rather, I'll do it for fun and writing potentially useful content for passersby to read and spend time on.

Think this site looks ugly?

This website marks my first time attempting a hand-written website, so expect, to an extent, that Elaboraet will look like a website from the early '00s for a while; however, I don't plan on using templates, pre-written frameworks (am I using that term correctly?) or themes or whatnot. For more advanced stuff, I am considering on implementing (and learning) JavaScript, though this may be in the distant future.

This entire site is about 2.6 MB on the NeoCities host. However, that doesn't necessarily mean your browser downloads 2.6 MB each time you visit. Using a site measuring tool, Elaboraet on NeoCities only stores about 68.0 KB (may vary slightly from browser to browser) on first visit to the homepage, indicating that only 68 kilobytes are being requested on first visit to the homepage.
