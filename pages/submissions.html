Article Requests and Submissions

Requests

Elaboraet is open to Article Requests. Mail your request to me here.

Submissions

Submissions must follow these basic guidelines:

  • Title, Author and Date Written
  • White OR Subtly Patterned Background
  • Conforms to Essay-Style Writing

The above guidelines are here to help any submissions fit into the rest of Elaboraet's aesthetic.

If you've written an article but don't know how to or cannot provide a formatted HTML, feel free to email me the text for me to format it to your needs for it to be published on Elaboraet.
