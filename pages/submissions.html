Article Requests and Submissions

Requests

Elaboraet is open to Article Requests.


Submitting An Article

If you're providing an article, its HTML file should

  • Have a title, author and date,
  • Have a white or subtly-patterned, non-intrusive background,
  • Be composed mostly of text with no extraneous pictures
  • Generally conform to essay-style writing.

The above guidelines are here to help keep Elaboraet both aesthetically streamlined and consistent in writing style.

If you can't provide an HTML file but can provide the writing itself, you can still email the submission. It will be formatted and published according to the above guidelines.

NSFW, highly shocking or otherwise unlawful submissions won't be published.
