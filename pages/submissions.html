Article Requests and Submissions

Requests

Elaboraet is open to Article Requests~ You can email it to me here for consideration.

Submissions

If you're providing an HTML, your submission must follow three basic guidelines:

  • Title, Author and Date Written
  • White OR Subtly Patterned Background
  • Conforms to Essay-Style Writing

The above guidelines are here to help any submissions fit into the rest of Elaboraet's aesthetic.

If you're providing the text only, email to the address above in order for formatting to be done for publishing.
